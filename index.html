About

Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal (SVKM) is a professionally managed educational trust established in the year 1934 with the objective of providing quality education to students. Over the decades, the trust has grown to include several prestigious educational institutions and is known for its commitment to academic excellence, discipline, and ethical values.

Narsee Monjee College of Commerce and Economics, established in 1964, functions under SVKM and focuses on holistic development of learners by combining academic rigor with ethical foundations. The college aims to prepare students for professional success and responsible citizenship.

Examination integrity is a vital component of the academic framework. Fair evaluation practices, adherence to examination rules, and ethical conduct during examinations ensure transparency and credibility in the assessment process.

This website has been developed as an awareness initiative to educate students about examination rules, examination offences, and the importance of honesty and discipline during examinations.
